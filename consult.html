The Work

What a Practice
Engagement Looks Like

Every practice is different. But most engagements touch the same core areas — the ones that have the highest impact on revenue and patient retention.
🔍
Practice Audit

We start by understanding exactly where the gaps are — from the first patient call through to post-treatment follow-up. No assumptions, just a clear picture of what's working and what isn't.

  • Patient journey mapping
  • Case acceptance rate analysis
  • No-show and cancellation pattern review
  • Communication touchpoint audit
⚙️
System Design & Build

Based on what we find, I design and implement the coordination systems your practice needs — intake protocols, treatment presentation frameworks, follow-up pipelines, and CRM setup.

  • Intake and onboarding protocol
  • Treatment plan presentation framework
  • HubSpot or CRM pipeline setup
  • Follow-up and reactivation sequences
👥
Staff Training

I don't just build the system — I train your team to run it. Written protocols, hands-on sessions, and documentation that survives staff turnover and keeps the standard consistent.

  • Role-specific coordination training
  • Patient communication scripting and coaching
  • Written onboarding protocol (keeps working after I leave)
  • Financial presentation technique training
📈
Ongoing Support

Available for ongoing advisory, quarterly reviews, and system refinements as your practice grows. The goal is to make your operation self-sustaining — not dependent on me being there.

  • Monthly or quarterly check-ins
  • Case acceptance and no-show tracking
  • System refinement as the practice scales
  • Staff coaching and protocol updates
